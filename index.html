A few words about me

It all started when I was in high school. Back then, programming looked like something scary and kind hard to spot it in your real life. Complex data structures and algorithms were all over the place and backtracking, divide et impera or oriented graphs were a pain for a young and unexperienced individual. Everything changed in college. At that point programming becamed something that looked like it has a real purpose. C++ becamed the key I needed to control different kind of robots and devices or to perform quality enhancement or watermarking algorithms on medical images. In college I've also discovered web development and I realised that's what I want to do next...

Like most web developers, I've started with HTML and CSS. I played around for a while and then I've discovered WordPress. At that point it felt like something new and interesting. I've created a few websites, most of them based on free themes and small code updates where I needed that. I've also started to learn SEO and after a while I've managed to create and maintain a few live websites that I owned at that point.

In 2015 I've decided to take a new challenge and become a member of the tech department at the company I'm still working for. We're talking about advertising, so the final goal was to create a tech stack that maximizes the revenue. Billions of sold ads later and dozens of happy clients I could say I've learned a lot.

Along with that, I've continued my freelancing experience and integrated a lot of new technoligies in my projects. My focus was on MERN Stack (MongoDB, ExpressJS, ReactJS, NodeJS), but that was just the backbone.
